January 13, 2023

EXPERIMENTAL FINANCE WORLD MEETINGS - SEF

The World Meetings of the Society for Experimental Finance 2023 will be held in Sofia. With a deadline for paper submissions on March 12, this anticipated event offers an opportunity for scholars and industry professionals to exchange cutting-edge research and ideas. Attendees can anticipate engaging discussions, thought-provoking presentations, and valuable networking in Sofia's captivating setting. SEF 2023 aims to advance the field of experimental finance by exploring topics such as behavioral finance, market microstructure, and risk management. Join us in Sofia for an unforgettable experience of knowledge sharing and collaboration.

February 25, 2021

VIRTUAL SUMMER SCHOOL

Attention all PhD students and advisors! The Experimental Finance Summer School is now open for enrollment. We are seeking a highly selective cohort of students, postdocs, and junior faculty interested in gaining expertise in experimental methods within finance. Esteemed instructors, including Stefan Nagel, Sebastien Pouget, Mark DeSantis, and Felix Holzmeister, will lead the program. Joining them will be experienced researchers, such as Zwetelina Iliewa, offering valuable interaction opportunities. Prepare for an engaging, hands-on experience that extends beyond the two-day school period. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity!

2020 SEF NORTH AMERICAN MEETINGS

ULEEF together with the Society of Experimental Finance hosted the 2020 SEF North American Meetings! The event was a success, if only judging by the number of people asking when again! This was two days of most insightful talks, debates, socials, and great support of our endeavor to bring experiments closer to the core of finance. A big thank you to the David Eccles School of Business, Rand Labs, Whitehall Ventures, and the Marriner Eccles Institute!
